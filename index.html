Blake Jones
About

I build production organizations that scale.

12+ years across integrated agency environments, most recently as EP and Head of Production Operations at Mekanism — where I launched and now lead the agency's AI Superstudio, one of the first AI-native production capabilities at a full-service creative agency.

My work sits at the intersection of creative ambition and operational rigor. I've overseen end-to-end campaign production across TV, digital, social, OOH, and performance media for brands including Amazon, NBA, Peloton, Sling TV, Frida, Alaska Airlines, and others. I manage multi-million dollar budgets, build vendor rosters, staff cross-functional teams, and create the workflows that let big ideas actually ship.

What I care about most: production as a strategic function, not just an execution layer. The best production leaders don't just run shoots — they shape how organizations think about making things, then build the infrastructure to do it better every time.

Currently pursuing my MBA at UC Berkeley Haas, focused on the intersection of marketing strategy, operations, and AI — and building fluency with the tools reshaping production, from OpenAI's generative models to custom AI pipelines built to move at creative speed.
